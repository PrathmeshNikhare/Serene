🧠 CBT · COGNITIVE RESTRUCTURING
That weight you're carrying sounds really heavy. Let's look at that thought together — "I'm going to fail and disappoint everyone." Can you tell me: what's the evidence for this thought? And what evidence might contradict it?
I've been studying 10 hours a day... I guess I do know the material.
🌱 POSITIVE REFRAME
Exactly. Ten hours every day — that's not someone who's going to fail, that's someone who cares deeply and has prepared. The anxiety is coming from that same care. It's not a sign of weakness — it's a sign you're invested. 💚
