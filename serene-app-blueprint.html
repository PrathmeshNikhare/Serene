Your unique edge: You are both the Engineer AND the Psychologist. Most mental health apps are built by engineers who hired psychologists. You understand both worlds natively. That is your moat.

AI-Powered Magic

🤖
Aura — AI Companion
Conversational AI trained on CBT, DBT, mindfulness. Detects emotional tone, responds with clinical empathy. Never replaces a therapist — always a safe first step. Escalates to human when crisis signals detected.
📊
Mood Intelligence Engine
Tracks mood patterns across days, weeks, months. Identifies triggers (bad sleep, exams, periods, Mondays). Sends proactive "You might need support today" nudges before the user even notices a dip.
🎯
Personalization Engine
Day 1 onboarding quiz builds a psychological profile. Recommends sessions, exercises, and content uniquely suited to each user's stress type, learning style, and current life phase.
🌐
Multilingual AI
Hindi, English, and regional languages. Mental health support in one's mother tongue reduces cognitive barrier and emotional distance — critical for rural and semi-urban India.
🚨
Crisis Detection AI
NLP monitors journal entries and conversations for distress signals, suicidal ideation, or acute crisis. Triggers immediate human intervention and shows helpline resources. Safety-first architecture.
📈
Progress Intelligence
Tracks clinically-validated scales (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS) monthly. Shows users their measurable improvement. Nothing drives retention like seeing your own numbers improve over time.
